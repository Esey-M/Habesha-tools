Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you use Habesha Tools, we collect:

  • Images you upload for processing
  • Usage data (e.g., features used, time spent on site)
  • Device information (e.g., browser type, operating system)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process your images as requested
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements

3. Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic
  • Serve personalized advertisements

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits and other sites.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Regular security assessments
  • Automatic deletion of processed images after 7 days

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Page.
